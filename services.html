Senior research leadership without a full-time hire. Research Roadmap Development

Translating scientific uncertainty into structured, executable plans. This includes scoping research questions, identifying methodological approaches, sequencing work against business or publication timelines, and defining what "done" looks like. Grounded in experience taking research from whiteboard to shipped product at Snowflake and CryptoNumerics.
